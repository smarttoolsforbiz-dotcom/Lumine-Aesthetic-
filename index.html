Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your treatments safe?

Absolutely. All our treatments are FDA-approved and performed by licensed medical professionals with extensive training. We use only medical-grade equipment and follow strict safety protocols. During your consultation, we'll review your medical history and discuss any concerns.

How much do treatments cost?

Pricing varies based on the treatment and areas being addressed. Botox starts at $12/unit, dermal fillers from $650/syringe, and laser treatments from $450/session. We provide detailed pricing during your free consultation and offer flexible financing options through CareCredit and Cherry.

Will treatments hurt?

Most treatments involve minimal discomfort. We use topical numbing creams, ice, and advanced techniques to ensure your comfort. Injectables feel like a small pinch, while laser treatments may feel like a rubber band snap. Most clients describe treatments as very tolerable.

How long do results last?

Results duration varies by treatment: Botox lasts 3-4 months, dermal fillers 9-18 months depending on type, laser treatments can be permanent for pigmentation and texture, and body contouring results are long-lasting with proper maintenance. We'll create a personalized maintenance plan for optimal results.

Is there any downtime?

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. Injectables may cause slight swelling or bruising for 1-2 days. Laser treatments typically involve 3-5 days of redness. Chemical peels have 5-7 days of peeling. We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions to minimize downtime.

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We partner with CareCredit and Cherry to offer flexible payment plans with 0% interest options for qualified applicants. You can apply online in minutes and receive instant approval. We also accept all major credit cards and offer package discounts for multiple treatments.

What should I expect during my consultation?

Your complimentary consultation takes about 30-45 minutes. We'll discuss your aesthetic goals, examine your areas of concern, review your medical history, and create a customized treatment plan with transparent pricing. There's no pressure or obligation – we want you to feel completely comfortable with your decision.

Can treatments be combined?

Absolutely! Many clients achieve optimal results by combining treatments. Popular combinations include Botox + fillers for full facial rejuvenation, laser + chemical peels for skin texture, and HydraFacial + microneedling for maximum glow. We'll recommend the best combination for your goals and budget.
